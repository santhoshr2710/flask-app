Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

"When your order is processed and ready for shipment, we'll promptly send you a confirmation email containing all the essential details of your purchase. This email will include a convenient tracking link, allowing you to monitor the journey of your package in real-time. Just a simple click on the provided link will keep you updated on your order's status, from dispatch to delivery. At Whookart, we prioritize your convenience and aim to keep you informed every step of the way through our email notifications."

What payment methods do you accept?

"At Whookart, we currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) as the sole payment method. We are working on introducing more payment options in the near future for added convenience. Stay tuned for updates on our expanding payment methods!"
